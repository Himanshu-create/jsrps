About Us

Jai Shree Ram Public School, established in 2010, has been a beacon of quality education, fostering academic excellence and holistic development. Affiliated with CBSE up to Class 8 and providing education up to Class 10, the school has built a strong reputation for nurturing young minds with a balanced curriculum of academics, sports, and co-curricular activities. Over the years, the institution has witnessed tremendous growth, both in student enrollment and the trust it has earned from parents and the community. With a dedicated team of highly qualified teachers, infrastructure, and a commitment to innovation in learning, the school ensures that every child receives the best educational experience. Jai Shree Ram Public School focuses on instilling strong moral values, leadership qualities, and critical thinking skills, preparing students for a bright future. The institution takes pride in its well-equipped classrooms, modern laboratories, a vast library, and ample sports facilities that promote overall personality development. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, discipline, and confidence, enabling them to excel in all walks of life.
